The

'the' is called definite article because it specifies the noun in particular.
Example:The dog is white(referring the particular dog that is white in colour)

Categories:

  • Used before singular countable nouns,plural countable nouns and uncountable nouns.
    Egs:the book, the books, the machines, the Iodine solution
  • Used when it is clear from the context which one we mean.
    Egs:The journal you were searching is here.
  • When a singular noun is meant to represent a whole class.
    Egs:The horse is a noble animal.
    The Baniyan is a kind of fig tree.
  • Used before some proper nouns.
    Egs:the Pacific,the Ganga,the Sahara,the West Indies,the Himalayas.
    The Vedas,the Sun,the Moon,the sea,the violin.
  • Used with ordinals
    Egs:He was the first man to arrive.
  • Used with superlatives
    Egs:The darkest cloud,the brightest scientist.
