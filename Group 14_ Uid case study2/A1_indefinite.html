A

'a' is an Indefinite article because it does not specify the noun. For Example: a scientist(referring any scientist)

Categories:

  • Used before singlur countable nouns. Egs:a voltmeter,a torsion balance.
  • Used before a word beginning with a consonant sound
    Egs:a women,a hole,a European,a University union,a unicorn,a useful article,a one eyed man,a one rupee coin
  • In its original numerical sense of 'one'
    Egs:12 inches make a foot.
  • Used in vague sense
    Egs:A technician came
  • Sometimes in rules and orders from a higher official or representative
    Egs:A pupil should obey his teacher

AN

'an' is an another Indefinite article because it refers any noun in random
For Example: an apple(referring any apple)

Categories:

  • Used before singlur countable nouns.
    Egs:An Ammeter,an electron microscope.
  • Used before a word beginning with a vowel sound.
    Egs:an orange,an umberella,an hour,an heir, an hotel ,an historical novel
  • Used in vague sense
    Egs:Buy an apple for your sister.
